The Puck Times

The Puck Times is a project I'm pursuing with my younger brother Andrew. It is a daily(ish) newsbrief covering the San Jose Sharks. We scan the universe of hockey news and concisely curate the highlights into a digestible format, and deliver it to users' inboxes every morning before each Sharks game.

I do not have any other 'active' projectes to report on at this time. That's not entirely true, but moreso I'm busy enough that I don't have time to document them all here. If you're interested in learning more, drop me a line at bbensch@gmail.com.
